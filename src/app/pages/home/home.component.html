We are non- profit-making organisation and we rely on volunteer contributions to carry out the limited activities that empower vulnerable youth including those living with disabilities.

Who are we

Youth Wisdom Foundation is an organisation which strives to educate the youth on various sustainable skills, environmental preservation and promote healthy lifestyles

Vision

An empowering and enabling environment where vulnerable youths have access to sustainable tools for development.

Mission

To become an impactful youth empowerment organisation in Zimbabwe and beyond by the year 2030.
